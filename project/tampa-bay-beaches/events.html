Beach Events

The Tampa Bay Area Beaches is always active with events all throughout the year. With a wide variety of events, you can plan your vacation around some of our upcoming beach events to get the most out of your vacation.
  • Saturday
    May
    4 9:00am

    Azalea Community Fresh Market

    7701 22nd Ave. N, St. Petersburg, 33707

    This new market in St. Petersburg's Tyrone area offers fresh produce, local and handcrafted foods, local artist's crafts and entertainment.

    Price: $0

  • Sunday
    May
    5 6:59am

    Police Appreciation Run/Walk

    Fifth Avenue NE and Bayshore Drive, St. Petersburg, 33701

    In honor of the lives of Officer Jeff Yaslowitz, Sergeant Tom Baitinger and Officer Dave Crawford, Lorraine Yaslowitz has established the Partners for Life Police Appreciation Run/Walk.

    Price: $25-$40

  • Monday
    May
    6 10:00am

    Garden Club Crafts Workshop: Memory Jar

    500 Sunset Drive S, St. Petersburg, 33701

    Get creative in Garden Club of St. Petersburg's craft workshop. This month's crafts is making "remembrance jars". This is an event for the whole family.

    Price: $4.50

  • Friday
    May
    10 6:00pm

    Bella Voce

    333 First St. S, St. Petersburg, 33701

    The black tie optional formal gala includes a reception, wine dinner, auctions, gift bag and entertainment by the St. Petersburg Opera. VIP tickets also include early entrance.

    Price: $50 (after hours), $125 general, $200 (VIP)

  • Saturday
    June
    1 8:08pm

    Couples Night

    10 Pier 60 Dr. Clearwater, FL 33767

    Bring your significant other to the Clearwater Beach for a romantic night of fun and pleasure. Enjoy live performances as the sun goes down as you sneak a kiss or two.

    Price: $0
